Spreading Pawsitivity

What is Pawsitivity?

Here at Pawsitivity, we strive to help high school students conquer their stress and worries.

Whether you are feeling pressure from final exams, AP classes, college apps, or school in general, you can rely on a dog's company to alleviate all of those negative feelings.

Our website focuses on utilizing dogs and canine-assisted therapy to relieve the stress of high school students and spread pawsitivity!
